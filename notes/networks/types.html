Types of Networks

  • LAN: Local Area Network within office/campus.
  • WAN: Wide Area Network (e.g., Internet).
  • MAN: Metropolitan Area Network (city-wide).
  • PAN: Personal Area Network (Bluetooth, Hotspot).
